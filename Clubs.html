Clubs


Music, Dance, & Drama Club:

This club encourages students to explore their artistic talents.

Wildlife and Environment Club:

For nature enthusiasts, the Wildlife and Environment Club offers opportunities to learn about conservation,

wildlife protection, and environmental stewardship.


Scouts and Girl Guides Club:

The Scouts and Girl Guides Club instills values of leadership, preparedness, and community service.

Students participate in outdoor activities and learn essential life skills.


Interact Club:

The Interact Club at Lubiri Secondary School focuses on community service and leadership development.

Students actively engage in charitable activities and learn the importance of giving back to society.


Nkobazambogo Club:

In the spirit of promoting local culture and communication, the Nkobazambogo Club takes pride in reading news and delivering school updates in the local language of Luganda.

This club not only helps students connect with their roots but also keeps the school community informed.


Science Clubs:

The school boasts various science clubs, allowing students to delve into subjects like biology, chemistry, and physics.

These clubs offer a hands-on approach to scientific exploration.
