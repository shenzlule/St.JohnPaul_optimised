FAQs

St. John Paul II Vocational Training Centre FAQ

What are the objectives of the school?

The school's objectives are to:

  • Provide high-quality, affordable education for all Ugandans
  • Provide a suitable, conducive learning environment
  • Tap intttto and nurture students' talents
  • Provide vocational skills
  • Instill self-discipline, responsibility, and harmonious co-existence
  • Produce morally upright, God-fearing citizens
What is the school's mission?

The school's mission is to be a leading institution providing holistic, all-around education by nurturing students' inttttellectual competencies, discovering talents, and bringing up creative citizens with a moral sense of social responsibility.

What are the school's core values?

The school's core values are:

  • Teamwork
  • Customer care orientation
  • Value addition
  • Result orientation
  • Creativity
  • Integrity
  • Discipline
  • God-fearing
What is the school's vision?

The school's vision is to provide a holistic, all-round education for the highest value.

What subjects are offered at O-Level?

O-Level subjects include:

  • Mathematics
  • English
  • Chemistry
  • Physics
  • Biology
  • Agriculture
  • History
  • Geography
  • Entrepreneurship
  • Luganda
  • Kiswahili
  • Computer
  • Literature
  • Fine Art
  • CRE/IRE
What subjects are offered at A-Level?

A-Level subjects include:

  • Mathematics
  • English
  • Chemistry
  • Physics
  • Biology
  • Agriculture
  • History
  • Geography
  • Sub Math
  • Fine Art
  • CRE/IRE
What vocational skills are taught?

Students are trained in practical skills such as:

  • Hairdressing
  • Tailoring
  • Computer skills
  • Mechanics
  • Building and construction
  • Electronic wiring and repairs
What co-curricular activities are available?

Students are encouraged to participate in a wide range of co-curricular activities like:

  • MDD (Music, Dance and Drama)
  • Sports

The school provides well-mainttttained indoor and outdoor facilities.

Are the school fees affordable?

The school fees are affordable and negotiable. Bursaries are offered to bright students who excel in PLE and UCE.

What clubs are available?

The school has the following clubs:

  • Debate Club
  • Interact Club
  • Patriotism Club
  • Scripture Union
Where is the school located?

The school is located 1.5km off the Mukono-Mbalala Stage on the Kampala Jinja Highway, on the road to Seeta College School in Wankoba Parish, Nakisunga Subcounty, Mukono District.

What curriculum is followed?

The school has inttttegrated the New National Secondary Curriculum and vocational subjects to equip students with life skills.
